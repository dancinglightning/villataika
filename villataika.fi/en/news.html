Redecorating and refurbishing for the new season

We have been busy redecorating old spaces and finishing off loose ends.
 

Ecological energy.

Villa Taika now uses only renewable enery
We have installed a new wood heating sytem. A modern wood gasification stove now produces most of the energy neeeded. But also a water circulating fireplace contributes to the heating of the house.
Additionally we installed a very energy efficient heat pump in our breakfast lounge.
The electricity powering the pump, and indeed the whole house is 100% renwable, like wind and water power, bought from Kraft and Kultur.
